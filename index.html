About Us

The sole purpose of Nalli Pte Ltd is to satisfy the demand for traditional Indian ethnic wear in Singapore. All Products in our stores are meticulously hand picked by the Directors, Managers and Staff of the company. We specialise in Silk Sarees and therefore, take pride in our selections as each individual/unique Saree is a reflection of the rich tradition and culture of various Regions of India. A variety of styles ranging from Prints, Embroideries, Weaves and others are available for sale. Moreover, Traditional, Modern and Designer Silk Sarees are also always in stock to cater to the discerning customer. Over the years of business, we have also gathered more knowledge and understanding about the tastes, likes and dislikes of the Singaporean market and our loyal customer base. Equipped with this information, all our products are methodically chosen and priced to satisfy our customers demands.

We recognise that a significant portion of the company’s sales attributes to Wedding/Bridal Customers’ needs. Privileged that we have a hand in the traditional proceedings of our Customers’ Weddings, we have procured all Products at Nalli Pte Ltd only on auspicious dates as a Thank you and Blessing to our customers.

Highlights

  • 1990

    Nalli Pte Ltd was founded by Nalli Duraisami Chetty who is the son of famous Nalli Chinnasami Chetty

  • 1991

    Opened a stall in front of then Ladies Centre (Chander Road)

  • 1992

    Opening of First Nalli Shop in Singapore at 27 Campbell Lane

  • 1999

    Opening of Second Nalli Shop in Singapore at 10 Buffalo Road

  • 2003

    Closure of 27 Campbell Lane shop due to impending pedestrianisation works of the Lane.

  • 2004

    In-house manufacturing of Ladies’ Tops and Readymade Blouses

  • 2010

    Customized procurement of Wedding Sarees

  • 2017

    Opening of Aajana Imitation Jewellery section on the 2nd Floor of 10 Buffalo Road

  • 2020

    Opening of next Nalli Shop in Singapore at 2C Buffalo Road
