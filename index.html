About Kosa

History of Kosa Silk Woven in the Vidarbha part of Maharashtra, Kosa is a more refined version of Tussar silk. Tussar silk sarees are one of the foremost popular sarees in India, much loved for their beauty, comfort, and sturdiness. These beloved Indian sarees are produced in Chhattisgarh,a state which is located in the center of India, known for its soft feel and texture, and it's purity, It is available naturally in shades of pale golden, dark honey, orange, fawn, cream, and many more. From the original color, the finished product is dyed using natural dyes from the flowers. Today Tussar silk is produced in cities like Raipur and Champa but traditionally, this exclusive silk fabric was only produced by the Dewangan tribe.
